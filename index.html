About

The Arts' Circle is a community of passionate, innovative and creative youth with a dream of taking the African Creative Industry to a whole new level. We are made up of a strong team of young authentic African ladies and gentlemen, who are not only focused on making their life better but also enabling the African Youth to be independent and make good use of their talents and abilities.

What We Do

Our duty is to create,market,sell and promote arts and creatives. We provide an ample environment for members to enhance their skills and use them for sustenance. We provide resources to talented artists who are unable to obtain by themselves and nurture new creative skills for future sustainability. These involves provision of paints and drawing materials for visual artists, linking creative writers to online writing sites, informing and connecting powerful actors to available opportunities and identifying and connecting dancers to form powerful crews. All the art creators linked to The Arts' Circle have an opportunity to market and sell their products. The arts' Circle's Photographers can be hired to cover events, our visual artists can be hired to make portraits and paints, our social media influencers are also available for online marketing and advertisement opportunities . We also design, make and sell African themed outfits and home decor materials. In the Spirit of Ubuntu, we also hold social events and visit schools to educate children and the youth on the importance of using arts and creativity for not only their own benefit but also the community.
